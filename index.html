Shiv Om Enterprises

Shiv Om Enterprises stands out as a leader in the foundry industry, building upon a strong foundation established in 2019. We strategically acquired a group with a proven track record of excellence in the Tractor & Automotive industry, particularly for precision machined components and Iron castings. This strategic move allowed Shiv Om Enterprises to inherit a wealth of experience and a well-established clientele. Their unwavering commitment to quality remains at the forefront of their mission. By adhering to international quality standards, we consistently deliver exceptional products at competitive prices. This focus on excellence has positioned us as the go-to supplier for a wide range of precision-engineered components across diverse sectors, including Tractor, Automotive, Commercial Vehicle, and other critical engineering industries worldwide.
